Welcome to this Monopoly simulator. The purpose of this simulator was to
evaluate the effectiveness of teaching artificial intelligence to play the
game of Monopoly. That research has now concluded. You can still use this
simulator to compete against three other computer players that are guided by
artificial intelligence. However, since the research is complete, you are
encouraged to play Monopoly online at Pogo.com (see the link below).
