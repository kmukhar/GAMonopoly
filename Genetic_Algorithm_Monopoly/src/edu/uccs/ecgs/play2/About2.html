Welcome to this Monopoly simulator. The purpose of this simulator was to evaluate the effectiveness of teaching artificial intelligence to play the game of Monopoly. That research has now concluded. You can still use this simulator to compete against three other computer players that are guided by artificial intelligence. However, since the research is complete, you are encouraged to play Monopoly online at Pogo.com (see the link below), or to buy a digital version of the game for your favorite platform. There are links at the end of the game that you can use to buy the game.

If you have questions or comments, please send an email to gamonopoly@gmail.com.
