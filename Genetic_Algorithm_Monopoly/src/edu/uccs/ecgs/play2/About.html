Welcome to this Monopoly simulator. The purpose of this simulator is to evaluate the effectiveness of teaching artificial intelligence to play the game of Monopoly. You the Human player will be competing against three other computer players that are guided by artificial intelligence.

After the game is complete, you will be asked to email a results file to the author so that the ability of the AI players can be evaluated. Please email those results when asked to do so.
